DAMN RATS

  • High Concept: Avoid the rats.
  • Theme: Run away from the rats.
  • Mandated Variety: On screen buttons. It lets the player to move left, right, and down.
  • Player Experience Goals: Challenging gameplay. It's hard to not touch the rats
  • Gameplay: You move by clicking "LEFT" or "RIGHT" button to go left or right. "DOWN" button is to go down. Remember to avoid the rats.
  • Prototype Goal: To see how hard the game is.
  • Features: The challenge of the level is quite hard from avoid the rats to doing it all with just on screen buttons.
  • Setting/Premise: There was a caveman. He is afraid of rats and just want to run away from them.
  • Story: Caveman named Bob has been through a lot his whole life. Though when he was 5 years old, a big rat appeared and start attacking his village. A lot of people lost their life during that night. Bob however, he witnessed his family murder in front of him. The rat ripped them apart and he has been afraid of rats since.
  • Target Audience: Those that is single but anyone can also feels the love.
  • Play Time: It could be very fast or it could takes up to 3-5 minutes.
  • Strategies: Don't take the rats
