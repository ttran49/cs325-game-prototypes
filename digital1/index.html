Time Saviors

  • High Concept: Earth is in danger, destroy the asteroid.
  • Theme: There is an asteroid that is heading toward Earth. Ride your ship and destroy it.
  • Mandated Variety: This is an arcade games with simple playstyle and idea.
  • Player Experience Goals: Accomblishment since you have saved our planet from another extinction.
  • Gameplay: Player can control the ship with left and right arrow which are the directions the ship will follow. The ship flies itself which mean the player doesn't have to hold down left/right arrow key to move. To unleash the power of the ship however, players would want to hold down spacebar. To fire, if players hold down spacebar, the ship will fire continuously in front of it. On the other hand to fire one shot, simply just tap spacebar once. Players can move and fire at the same time. Combining this, destroying the asteroid and saving Earth is not an impossible task anymore.
  • Prototype Goal: The prototype tells the main concept and story that the game conveys. Furthermore, it gives an early experience of how the game feels and played.
  • Features: A unique story and the potential to grow the game up. The playstyle is simple, easy to learn but the gameplay could be very intense based on how the level is designed.
  • Setting/Premise: The world is different as time changes. You're from the future and determined to make it better. All of the terible events are boiled down to an asteroid that collided with Earth 20 years before the character was born.
  • Story: You're an agent for an agency with the mission of making the world a better place by alternating the past. With time travelling technology and knowledge of advanced technology, this task doesn't seem to be impossible. All the wars, conflicts, and deaths of billions people are caused by the collision of an asteroid with Earth. Now with the technology, the agency plans to send back a team to assemble a ship with the capability to fly to space, find the asteroid, and destroy it. So far, the team has succeed to create the ship, your mission is to find the destroy the asteroid. After flying to where the asteroid is, can you destroy it and save humanity? Each stages of the story could be a level with challenges that the players have to overcome to beat the level. There could be many level and could build on forever. The story can also expands to after the players destroy or not destroy the asteroid.
  • Target Audience: Audiences that don't have much time to play games. This game designs for players who just want to kill 20 minutes while they are waiting for something.
  • Play Time: The game is fast. About 2 or 3 minutes to destroy the asteroid. After all, the asteroid is no match to our superior technology. Furthermore, other levels could take 4-5 minutes to beat.
  • Strategies: Destroy everything, save the Earth.
