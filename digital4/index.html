Shootin Spongebob

  • High Concept: Try to hit Spongebobs.
  • Theme: Spongebobs are going crazy, calm them down by touching them with a special pearl.
  • Mandated Variety: On screen buttons. It lets the player to control the ball left/up and right/up.
  • Player Experience Goals: Challenging gameplay. The way the ball is moving is unsual therefore it's harder to control and hit Spongebobs.
  • Gameplay: You move by clicking "LEFT" or "RIGHT" button to go left or right.
  • Prototype Goal: To see how hard the game is.
  • Features: The challenge of the level is quite hard to control the ball by just with on-screen buttons.
  • Setting/Premise: Spongebobs got mad once day and only calm down with the touch of a special pearl.
  • Story: There was an orb where its special ability is calm whoever it touches. One day, because of the annoying neighbors, Spongebobs got mad and went crazy. Use the orb to calm them down.
  • Target Audience: Whoever that like challenging games.
  • Play Time: It could be very fast or it could takes up to 3-5 minutes.
  • Strategies: Go slowly, don't spam the ball one way.
